What We Do


Mission

Allegra's mission is to seamlessly immerse users into a personalized language learning environment, guiding them one step closer to fluency.

Purpose

This humble resource is dedicated to helping learners who want to escape the rigid structure of memorization and establish natural fluency using music.

Technology

Through state-of-the-art tools like pinecone vectorization, machine learning, NLP, Flask servers, linux hosting, GPT3 text embedding, GPT4 whisper2, and an sqlite dynamic server, Allegra AI was developed with the user's language experience in mind.

Languages

Allegra AI was created through languages primarily including HTML, CSS, SCSS, Javascript, Python, PythonDB, Bootsrap, OpenAI, and other APIs.

What is Allegra AI

"Through the lyrics of a song, we glimpse the soul of a language, its beauty resonating in every verse."

In today's interconnected world, learning a language is in higher demand than ever, and there's no better platform to improve one's language skills than with Allegra AI. Allegra AI allows the user to upload their favorite songs in their target language, and answer practice questions regarding the grammatical structure and vocabulary of the lyrics. Using a page ranking algorithm, the application ranks users against each other to compete for the top spot in the Allegra.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of language learning through immersion?

Language learning encourages natural acquistion of a language through intuition, and increases the user's cultural understanding and perspectives of native speakers.

Why songs? Why not another form of media?

By utilizing the user's favorite songs, its catchy melodies contain lots of repetition which aids in vocabulary retention and pronunciation practice. It also allows for full immersion into the natural language patterns, idomatic expressions, and colloquisms of the target language.

Why should I use Allegra AI over another language learning platform?

Allegra AI has the unique characteristic of language learning through immersion and repetition. Instead of sitting through tiring and monotonous book exercises that do not reflect real-life communication, Allegra offers a reinforcing language acquisition by supplementing listening skills with grammatical exercises.

How does vectorization work in the context of this app?

Allegra chunks the lyrics of the song uploaded by the user and upserts the chunks into the vector database. After the user completes the tasks from the song, the user has the option of querying the grammatical concept they would like to focus on. From there, the DB conducts a similarity search from Spanish textbooks and passes the top quotes to OpenAI API to create questions for the user.
